Welcome to the Myrtle Beach Table Tennis Community!

Okay, so what is this?

This site is to help build a fun and competitive Table Tennis community in the Myrtle Beach and surrounding area. The Myrtle Beach Table Tennis Club is our home base and where most of the community is based out of. You can find information about our club and other places to play in the area on this site.

Nice! Why Table Tennis?

Table Tennis has been an Olympic Sport since 1988. It is a world-wide growing sport that players of all ages and athletic ability can enjoy. Table Tennis is a low-impact sport that improves hand-eye coordination, footwork and brain function. There is only 9 feet between players
